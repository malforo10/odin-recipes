Guacamole

Description

Make a traditional mexican Guacamole with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma tomatos
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  3. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
