Pico de Gallo

Description

A fresh and chunky salsa made with tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and lime juice.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, mix diced tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and jalapeño.
  2. Stir in lime juice and salt; mix well.
  3. Chill for 15-20 minutes to enhance the flavor.
  4. Serve as a topping for tacos, burritos, or alongside chips.
