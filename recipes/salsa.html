Salsa

Description

A classic tomato-based salsa that is fresh, zesty, and perfect for chips or tacos.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeño, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes, onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and garlic in a large bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, salt, and cumin; mix well.
  3. Let the salsa sit for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for your favorite dishes.
